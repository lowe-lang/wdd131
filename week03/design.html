Good Design = Good Experience

Design Principles in Action

Typography

Limit font families to two or three. Ensure text is readable, consistent, and supports your brand’s tone.

  • Consistency: Maintain uniform color, font, and spacing.
  • Clarity: Avoid clutter—use whitespace to focus attention.
  • Visual Hierarchy: Use size and contrast to guide the eye.
  • Accessibility: Use sufficient color contrast and alt text for images.

Remember!

Design for users first—your layout, typography, and colors should support usability, not just style.
