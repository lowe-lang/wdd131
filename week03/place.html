About the Country

Federal Reublic Nigeria is located in West-Africa region. It's capital city is named "Abuja", and it's largest city is Lagos. Nigeria official language is English, and have other major language like, Hausa, Yoruba, and Igbo. It's population approximately to 223 million, and their currency is naira(#). Nigeria share borders with Benin(west), Niger(north), Chad(northeast), and Atlantic Ocean(south), it's area is about 923,769km, and have major rivers like, River Niger and River Benue. The Republic of Nigeria have a division of 36 states plus federal capital territory(Abuja), and its President name as of 2025 is Bola Almed Tinubu.
